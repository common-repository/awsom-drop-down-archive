Install guide for AWSOM Archive plugin

1) Unzip AWSOM archive zip file.
2) Upload awsomarchive folder to your wp-content/plugins folder on your webserver.
3) Activate AWSOM Archive in your Admin "Plugins" area.
4) Done!

Upgrade guide
1) Unzip AWSOM archive zip folder.
2) Deactivate current version on your site. Delete plugin files
3) Upload awsomarchive folder to your wp-content/plugins folder on your webserver.
4) Activate new version.
5) Done!
